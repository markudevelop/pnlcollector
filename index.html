Our Fund's Strategy

Our fund specializes in a high-probability 0 DTE (Zero Days to Expiration) options strategy on the S&P 500 Index (SPX). This approach is designed to systematically harvest premium by capitalizing on rapid time decay (theta). We do not guess market direction; our strategy manages probabilities to seek consistent, daily income for our partners.

The PNL Fund Advantage
